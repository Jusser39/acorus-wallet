# Acorus Character Bible

Date: 2026-05-24

## Character

**Name:** Acorus Guardian

**Role:** A friendly cyber-magic wallet guardian that visually represents local
custody, multichain routing, and transaction review.

## Visual Rules

- Palette: cyan, violet, pink, white glass, and small gold accents.
- Silhouette: compact guardian/orb-first mascot that can later be replaced by a
  full character illustration without changing layout.
- Mood: safe, intelligent, energetic, premium, and approachable.
- Environment: translucent glass panels, soft grid, portal glow, floating token
  chips, and clean fintech controls.
- Prohibited: copied wallet mascots, MetaMask fox likeness, Trust Wallet shield
  likeness, Uniswap branding, Pokemon/Disney/Genshin-like copyrighted designs,
  or any third-party logo recreation.

## Page Poses

- Home: neutral guardian floating above a platform with ETH, BTC, SOL, and USDC.
- Wallet: shield/portfolio guardian with balance and activity panels.
- Swap: alchemy pose exchanging two assets.
- Send: light-beam pose for outgoing transfers.
- Receive: portal pose catching incoming assets.
- Explore: atlas/map pose with market discovery panels.
- Security: shield pose with permission and vault controls.
- dApps: portal pose opening connected app cards.

## Asset System

- `AcorusMagicStage` is the code-native fallback stage.
- `AcorusMage` is the current local SVG/CSS mascot.
